Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

We offer refunds under specific circumstances to ensure fair treatment for all customers. Please review our policy carefully before making a purchase. All refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

1. Refund Policy

No refunds will be given unless there is an error or other cause, or if the item was delivered late, but it will depend on the circumstances.

All refund requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by our support team. We are committed to fair and transparent resolutions for all customers.

2. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Open a Ticket: Join our Discord server and open a support ticket
  2. Provide Information: Include your order ID, purchase date, and detailed explanation
  3. Cooperate with Support: Provide any requested information or logs
  4. Wait for Review: Our team will review your case within 24-48 hours
  5. Receive Decision: You will be notified of the decision via Discord or email
  6. Processing: If approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

Required Information:

  • Order ID or transaction reference
  • Purchase date and amount
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots or evidence (if applicable)
  • System specifications (for technical issues)

3. Partial Refunds

For subscription-based licenses, partial refunds may be issued based on unused time, minus a processing fee.

Calculation:

  • Refund amount = (Total paid / Total days) × Unused days - Processing fee (10%)
  • Minimum refund amount: $5.00
  • Partial refunds are only available for subscriptions of 1 month or longer

This is evaluated on a case-by-case basis and is not guaranteed.

4. Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:

  • Cryptocurrency: Refunded to the original wallet address (minus network fees)
  • Bank Transfer: Refunded to the original account within 5-7 business days
  • True Money: Refunded as wallet credit or original payment method

Note: We cannot refund to a different payment method than originally used.

5. Chargebacks

Warning: Initiating a chargeback without contacting support first will result in:

  • Immediate permanent ban from all services
  • Blacklisting of your HWID and payment information
  • No future purchases allowed
  • Potential legal action for fraud

Always contact our support team first to resolve any issues. We are committed to fair resolutions.

6. Service Downtime Compensation

If our service is unavailable for extended periods:

  • 24-48 hours: Subscription time automatically paused
  • 48-72 hours: Additional 1 day added to subscription
  • 72+ hours: Eligible for partial refund or extended subscription

Downtime compensation is applied automatically. No action required from users.

7. Contact Support

For any refund inquiries or issues, please contact our support team:

  • Discord: Open a support ticket (Fastest response)
  • Email: support@serif-signal02.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for all inquiries

We aim to resolve all refund requests fairly and promptly. Our support team is here to help!

This refund policy is designed to protect both our customers and our business. We strive for transparency and fairness in all transactions.
